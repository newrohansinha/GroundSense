# GroundSense

## Product
Executive intelligence platform for industrial/distribution companies. Maps external events into operating exposure: event → operating path → financial driver → dollar exposure → executive action.

## Register
product

## Audience
CFOs, COOs, procurement heads, and their chiefs of staff at mid-market industrial companies (~$500M–$5B revenue). Time-constrained, numbers-first, allergic to noise.

## Mission
Not a news summarizer. Every signal must trace to a dollar range and an owner action. Evidence-backed vs. scenario-modeled is always disclosed.

## Core surfaces
- Dashboard (executive intelligence home: KPIs, memo, actions, exposure graph)
- Risks (full issue register: risks, operating changes, watchlist, quality gate)
- Calibration Center (model inputs: freight, supplier, CRM, financial data)
- Source Hub (external signal sources and coverage)

## Stack
React + TypeScript, Supabase (Postgres + Edge Functions), Vite

## Brand direction
Authoritative, earned trust, no hype. Warm cognac/amber accent (#c2620a) against near-white light surfaces (or near-black dark surfaces). Inter for type. The accent is distinctive for fintech/executive tooling (avoids the default navy/blue lane).
